Affiliate Scheme for Resource

As we all know, talented team is backbone for any IT company. Based on past experience, we realized that, when we hire resource by reference (especially by any of you), it really work well. Because you understand our need better and when you refer candidate, there are 99% chances that he/she is right candidate for company. BlueSoft would like to promote the same by paying bonus to you when you do so.

So now on, whenever you refer any experienced resource to company and if company hire him/her, you will get bonus of 2,500 Rs. You should take care that you refer right candidate to the company as we spend good amount of time in interview process. When you refer right candidate, it saves time.

Terms & Conditions

– This scheme is exclusively for BlueSoft staff only (except HR Staff). Ex-BlueSoft employee is eligible for this scheme as well.

– You will get said bonus after new employee (referred by you) work with BlueSoft for 2 consecutive months.

– If BlueSoft decide to discontinue services of new employee before 2 months, you will not be eligible for said bonus amount.

– We use few consultants and some other ways for recruitment. So ideally you should send us cv or contact information of candidate on time whenever we have requirements. If we approach candidate by other mean before you refer him/her, your reference will not be considered. Same holds true if candidate approaches us before you refer him/her.

– This scheme is just applicable for recruitment of experienced resource and not for fresher or trainee recruitment. Only experience after graduation will be considered to decide one as experienced resource.

– Above mentioned terms shall be amended from time to time.

– This scheme is effective from 7 th May 2012.

Innovative knowledge contributor of the Quarter

According to this scheme, You can post/share your views, knowledge and ideas related to your technical Knowledge gained from your past experiences which can help others on NISL Forum. The staff member who will be getting maximum likes on his/her post and selected by management team based on material posted will be selected as the “Innovative knowledge contributor of the Quarter” As the title speaks “Innovative knowledge contributor of the Quarter” Winners will be declared and awarded Quarterly i.e. In first week of April (For Jan-Feb-Mar), July (For Apr-May-Jun), October (For Jul-Aug-Sep) and January (For Oct-Nov-Dec) of the Year. Winners will also be awarded with certificate and some kind of reward.

The winners of the rewards will be decided as per below:

1) As per the quality of material or content posted, which will be decided by Management.

2) Usability of the code or content by others.

3) Number of votes received.
